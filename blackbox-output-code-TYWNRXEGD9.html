Basics of Stock Market

  • Equity Trading: Buying/selling shares on NSE/BSE.
  • Indices: Nifty 50 (top 50), Banknifty (banks), Sensex (30 large-caps).
  • Market Hours: 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST.
  • Order Types: Market, Limit, Stop-Loss.

Options Trading Fundamentals

  • Calls/Puts: Calls for upside, puts for downside.
  • Strike, Expiry, Premium: Selection based on outlook.
  • Greeks: Delta (price sensitivity), Theta (time decay), Vega (volatility).
  • Intrinsic/Extrinsic Value: Real vs. time value.

Strategies for Profit

  • Buying Options: Limited risk, high reward.
  • Selling Options: Premium income, but unlimited risk.
  • Spreads: Bull Call, Bear Put, Iron Condor.
  • Hedging: Protect portfolios with options.

Technical Analysis

  • Chart Patterns: Head & Shoulders, Double Tops.
  • Indicators: SMA/EMA, RSI, Bollinger Bands.
  • Support/Resistance: Key levels for trades.
  • Candlesticks: Doji, Hammer for signals.

Fundamental Analysis

  • Ratios: P/E, P/B, ROE.

Risk Management & Psychology

  • Position Sizing: 1-2% risk per trade.
  • Stop-Loss: Fixed or trailing.
  • Emotional Control: Avoid FOMO.
  • Journaling: Track and improve.

Expiry Day Tactics: Zero to Hero Trades

  • Understanding Expiry: Options expire on last Thursday; time decay accelerates.
  • Zero-Hero Strategy: Buy cheap OTM options early, sell near expiry if in-the-money for massive gains (e.g., 10x returns).
  • Big Profits: Focus on high IV days; use gamma scalping or theta harvesting.
  • Stop-Loss Trailing: Set initial SL at 20% loss; trail up as profit grows (e.g., move SL to breakeven after 50% gain).
  • Examples: On expiry, if Nifty hits 22,200, sell calls bought at 21,800 for 5x profit. Always exit before 3 PM.
  • Risks: Avoid holding to expiry; use alerts for levels.
